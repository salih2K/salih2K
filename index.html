About The Developer

Salih - Unity Game Developer

Hello! I'm Salih, a passionate Unity game developer with over 5 years of experience creating immersive gaming experiences. I specialize in 3D game development, interactive storytelling, and optimizing gameplay mechanics.

My journey in game development started during my computer science studies, and since then I've been dedicated to mastering the art of creating engaging games that players love.

When I'm not coding, you can find me playing games for inspiration, learning new techniques, or exploring other creative outlets like digital art and music composition.

I believe that games are a powerful medium for storytelling and creating memorable experiences. Each game I develop is a new adventure that I'm excited to share with the world.
